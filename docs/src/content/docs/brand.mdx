## Clear space & minimum size

Give the mark room. Keep clear space on all sides at least equal to the height of the emblem's crossbar, and do not crowd it with text or other logos.

Minimum sizes, so the split-pillar **H** stays legible:

| Mark | Minimum |
| --- | --- |
| Emblem | 16px (favicon size) |
| Full logo | ~120px wide |
| Wordmark | ~140px wide |

## Usage

**Do**

- Use the supplied files as-is, and pick the treatment that suits the background (reversed or white on dark, primary or mono black on light).
- Keep the clear space above.
- Use the emblem alone when the full logo would be too small to read.

**Don't**

- Recolor, redraw, rotate, stretch, or add effects (shadows, outlines, gradients of your own).
- Recreate the mark in another font or swap the orange-to-teal gradient.
- Use the marks as your own product or app icon, or in any way that implies endorsement or affiliation.
